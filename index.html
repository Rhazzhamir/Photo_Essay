Author's Reflection

About the Author

Documenting this transformation through photography allowed me to appreciate the rapid changes in hardware design. Seeing the evolution of laptops firsthand, from bulky and rigid builds to sleek and lightweight models, highlighted how far technology has come. Capturing these differences through my lens gave me a new perspective on the impact of design choices on usability and aesthetics.

The challenge was capturing fine details, such as keyboard textures and screen differences, while maintaining consistent lighting and composition. Each shot required careful adjustments to ensure clarity and accuracy. Reflections on glossy screens, subtle changes in key layouts, and varying materials made it necessary to experiment with angles and lighting setups. The process taught me patience and precision in photography, emphasizing the importance of meticulous planning.

This project made me realize that technology doesn't just evolveâ€”it adapts to the way we live and work. Every design shift reflects changing user needs, from enhanced portability for remote work to improved ergonomics for long hours of use. Observing these transformations through photography reinforced how innovation is driven by real-world demands, shaping the tools we rely on daily.

- Rhazzhamir I. Aguilar
